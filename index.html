How I Think

My approach to AI

I'm not here to automate people out of their jobs. I'm here to take the repetitive, draining work off their plate so they can focus on what actually matters.

I've deployed AI tools across multiple companies — not as experiments, but as real integrated solutions that teams actually use. The technology is rarely the hard part.

The hard part is change management. It's helping a team trust a new workflow. It's making sure the solution fits how people actually work, not how we wish they worked. It's getting everyone across the line together.

That's where the engineering mindset meets the people side — and that combination is what makes adoption stick.
