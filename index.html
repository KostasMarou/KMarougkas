How I Think

I've deployed AI and automation tools not as experiments, but as real integrated solutions that teams actually use. The technology is rarely the hardest part.

The hard part is change management: helping teams trust a new workflow, making sure the solution fits how people actually work, and getting everyone across the line together.

That's where the engineering mindset meets the people side — and that combination is what makes adoption stick.
